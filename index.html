Sarah Test

Credit Gamer

Sarah sees credit cards as strategic financial tools instead of debt. She applies for them at the right time, keeps track of rewards and uses them strategically to earn travel perks. At the same time, she stays organized and protects her excellent credit score.

Goals

  • Earn luxury international travel using credit card rewards
  • Maintain a credit score above 750
  • Maximize sign-up bonuses and reward multipliers strategically

Frustrations

  • Buggy or poorly designed financial apps
  • Financial advice without clear data or math
  • Slow point transfers or confusing reward systems
